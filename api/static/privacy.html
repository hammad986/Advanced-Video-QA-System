Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. What We Collect

When you use Video-QA, we collect:

  • Account information — your email address and a hashed version of your password (we never store your password in plaintext).
  • Video and audio files — files you upload or YouTube URLs you submit for processing.
  • Transcripts and embeddings — machine-generated text derived from your media, used to power the Q&A search index.
  • Usage metadata — timestamps of uploads, processing status, and query logs stored per user account.

We do not collect browser fingerprints, tracking cookies, or advertising identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the Video-QA service:

  • AI processing — your audio is transcribed using a local speech-recognition model (Whisper). Transcripts are chunked, embedded, and stored in a local vector index so you can query them.
  • Q&A retrieval — your questions are compared against your video transcripts to generate grounded answers. Your queries are not used to train models.
  • Authentication — your email is used only to identify your account and, if you opt in, to send one-time verification codes.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.

3. Storage and Retention

  • Uploaded files are stored on the server (or an S3-compatible bucket) under your account.
  • Transcripts and vector embeddings are stored locally on the server alongside the file.
  • When you delete a video, all associated files, transcripts, and index entries are permanently removed.
  • When you delete your account, all data is purged.
  • We do not retain backups of user-deleted content beyond routine server snapshots (rotated every 7 days).

4. Third-Party Services

If you sign in with Google, your Google profile email and account ID are received and stored. We do not receive your Google password. Google's privacy policy governs their data handling.

If S3 storage is configured, files are transmitted to and stored by your configured cloud storage provider under your own credentials.

5. Security

All API endpoints require authentication. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. Bearer tokens are short-lived JWTs. Media files are served only to authenticated owners via signed or token-gated URLs.

No system is perfectly secure. Do not upload content that you cannot afford to have disclosed in the event of a breach.

6. Your Rights

  • You may delete any video at any time via the sidebar library.
  • You may request full account deletion by contacting the service operator.
  • You may export your transcripts by asking questions or using the API directly.

7. Children

Video-QA is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information, contact us so we can delete it.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be noted here with a revised date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions? Open an issue or contact the operator via the repository linked in the app.
